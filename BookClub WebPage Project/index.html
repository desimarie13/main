About the House of Wind Book Club

Based on the House of Wind from Sarah J. Maas' series, A Court of Silver Flames- this group came together with the love of reading, fantasy, and all things SJM. While our book club has focused on SJM's three series (A Court of Thorns & Roses, Crescent City, and Throne of Glass), we do enjoy a other authors in the fantasy genre, and delve outside the realm to romance, mystery, and more!

Meeting Schedule

We meet the second Saturday of every month to discuss our most recent book! What we liked, didn't enjoy as much, swooned for, plot twists, etc. And of course, to just have fun with fellow book lovers! Make sure you join our Facebook page to check for updates on times and locations!
